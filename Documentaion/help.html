How to change logo

you can use text in h2 tag

                                    
<!-- logo HTML markup start -->
<div class="col-md-3 col-sm-3">
    <div class="logo">
        <a> class="smooth" href="#header">
            <!-- you can use here img or he tag as you need -->
            <h2> ... your logo name/text ...</h2>
        </a>
    </div>
</div>
<!-- logo HTML markup END -->

or you can use img in img tag

                                    
<!-- logo HTML markup start -->
<div class="col-md-3 col-sm-3">
    <div class="logo">
        <a> class="smooth" href="#header">
            <!-- you can use here img or he tag as you need -->
            <img> src=" ... your logo image path ... " alt="logo">
        </a>
    </div>
</div>
<!-- logo HTML markup END -->
